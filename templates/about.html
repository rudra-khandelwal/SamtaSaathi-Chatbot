About Samta Foundation

Our Vision

Making rural and tribal communities healthy, literate, and self-reliant; and working with prisoners to improve their quality of life.

Our Mission

Ensure that aid reaches exactly where, when, and how it is needed and create value for our society by applying real-world solutions.

Our Philosophy

Founded in 2012, we work upward from the basics, creating systems designed with beneficiaries in mind. Our strength lies in consistency, planning programs at regular intervals to address real needs.

This ensures complete life-cycle management across our various initiatives, providing sustainable impact for the communities we serve.

Founder

Purushottam Agrawal

Founder and Principal Trustee

His innovative approach to social reform is at the heart of Samta Foundation.

He has been awarded by the Honourable Vice President of India with the prestigious Champions of Change 2018 Award, Social Activist, Maharashtra, for the work done in the Washim District, Maharashtra.
