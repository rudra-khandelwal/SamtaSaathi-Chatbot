Samta Foundation Officers

Samta Foundation is providing free services in rural and tribal areas across Maharashtra where there is limited access to healthcare services.
A key component of our program is cataract cure services, where our officers coordinate with local government authorities and hospitals to support patients through the entire Cataract Cure life cycle; from transportation to camps, eye checkups, surgery and post-operative care. If you know someone who can benefit from our cataract surveys and free cataract camps, reach out to any of our Samta Officers listed below.
